Nmap (Network Mapper)

Nmap is a powerful open-source tool used for network discovery and security auditing.

Working:
It sends packets to a target system and analyzes responses to determine open ports, services, and operating system details.

Command Used:
nmap -sV 192.168.154.129

Explanation:
- -sV: Detects service versions running on open ports.

Findings:
The scan identified multiple open ports such as:
- Port 21 (FTP)
- Port 22 (SSH)
- Port 80 (HTTP)

Security Risk:
Open ports increase attack surface and may allow unauthorized access if services are vulnerable or misconfigured.
